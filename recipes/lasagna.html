Lasagna

Description

This is a fantastic recipe for the whole family. It is sure to be a crowd pleaser (even for those picky kids!)

This usually takes me just under 60 minutes to cook, so its not too time consuming and most of it is the baking time :)

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • cheese
  • meat
  • tomato
  • more cheese :))

Steps

  1. mix pasta
  2. layer cheese and meat between sheets of pasta
  3. cook in oven for 30-35 mins
  4. when cheese looks golden and there is browning, take it out of oven
